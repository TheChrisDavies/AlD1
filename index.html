Hi, I'm Al.

I'm a Toronto-based freelance journalist, writer, and chronic dog petter. I report on Canadian news, culture, anti-oppression, video games, and independent arts.

I have bylines with Huffington Post Canada, Torontoist, Buzzfeed Canada, Vice Canada, CBC, This Magazine, Broken Pencil Magazine, and more.

Currently, I'm the Hand Eye Society's community manager and newsletter writer. I ramble about indie games in an ongoing column with Broken Pencil Magazine.
